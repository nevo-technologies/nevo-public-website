Nevo Design Partner Program

In short:
  • Nevo builds software solutions for its clients
  • We build many web-based solutions
  • We have no web design practice
  • We look to partner with great web design firms
Nevo's projects often involve web-based systems, and, as the expectations for the elegance of a web interface grow, our projects often include creative design work. Such work might be as small as a choice of colors and fonts or as large as a set of Photoshop mockups of most of the system's screens. At Nevo, we have chosen to focus our culture and our skills on building great software systems, and we believe that the artistic and visual orientation required for great design is quite a different skill set. We typically look to a partner for those skills and talents.

Occasionally, we work on projects initiated by a design agency that finds the implementation details of a site to be too technical for its staff. We appreciate such referral business. More often, we initiate the projects on our own. In such cases our clients often ask us to recommend a design agency to do the visual portion of the work. To allow us to respond appropriately, we started this program.
