Nevo technologists have collectively—and in some cases, individually—spent decades advancing the state of the art in software. Just as important, though, Nevo brings a depth of experience about business processes, about organizational needs, and most of all about people. We know that successful implementations are less about technology than about implementing the right system. And finding the right system to implement always depends upon carefully eliciting the requirements and addressing the expectations of diverse communities of interest. To leverage our experience and best practices, Nevo uses a proven, repeatable lifecycle process to ensure that we know what constitutes success, we know how to get there, we continually drive hard to achieve it, and we share our knowledge through active programs of mentoring and knowledge transfer.
