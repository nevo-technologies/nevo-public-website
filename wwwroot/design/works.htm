How It Works

Our partner program is informal and effective. To join, just click here and fill out the form. We collect some basic information about your firm for our partner database and refer to that database when we encounter a project that requires interactive design services. The information helps us pick out the projects that might be a good match for you. Once you submit the form, we'll give you call and set up a brief meeting so that we can each see firsthand what the other is about. There are no fees and no contracts; it's just a friendly referral arrangement.

We do write the occasional (approximately monthly) short article for our design partners. These articles are written for readers without extensive software development skills, and they're intended to keep you informed of the latest developments in web software frameworks and how those developments will impact your world. When we post a new one, we'll send you an email; if you're not interested, just let us know.
